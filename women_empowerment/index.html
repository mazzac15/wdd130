The purpose of WE:

Woman can achieve incredible goals. We can accomplish more than we may first assume to be possible. There are women who are creative, strong, independent, good leaders, unique, hard workers and the list goes on. By working TOGETHER, we can accomplish even more. It would be so incredible to have a place where women can support other women.

How can women support women? Here on WE there are message boards where we can ask questions and look for help. There are capable women and their businesses offering goods and services from construction to handmade crafts. We can support each other, we can collaborate together, and we can all come out stronger in the end.

Women Success Stories

Cocinando con Olga Youtube Channel

Olga started her Youtube Chanel in 2019. Sharing food with family and friends always brought her so much joy. Her family kept asking her to write down her recipes. This idea took form and she was able to share what she loved with so many people. Olga now has more than 1.3k followers and is getting ready to launch a Artesinal Alfajor line of the delicious Argentine cookies.

Paola's Blown Up Party Balloons

Paola had always wanted to start her own business but for quite some time she wasn't sure what business she could create. After helping family prepare for some birthday parties, she realized that she had a talent in creating beautiful birthday backdrops from balloons. She saw an opportunity to be her own boss and do something she enjoyed. Paola finally took the chance and started her business this year. Way to go Paola!

Veronica with Vready Designs

Veronica has been a stay at home mom for the last 10 years. She has a passion for beauty products and tutorials which led to her product creation. She had seen many tips and tricks to highlight the nose and new there had to be a better way. In a short amount of time she created a product that works to guide someone to add contour and highlights in an easy and effective way. Way to use your passion to create a new product!
